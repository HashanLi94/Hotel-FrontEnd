About us

Follow the sunshine to Del Beach and relax into the gentle ocean waves and sparkling sands of our classic California beach resort. From roasting s’mores with your toes in the sand to drifting away into an ocean-inspired spa treatment or relaxing on a luxurious Del Beach daybed, life is better at The Del.

Whether you prefer the historic charm of the Victorian building, the contemporary Ocean Towers and California Cabana buildings, or the luxurious Beach Village, our sprawling 28-acre resort has rooms and suites to suit your style. Please reserve the type of room you wish to stay in upon arrival as upgrades may be limited.

Seven seaside dining experiences. Locally sourced ingredients. Expert chefs. Discover San Diego’s best restaurants overlooking the Pacific.

Location

Hotel del Coronado is the quintessential beachfront Coronado, CA hotel resort destination. Nestled on 28 stunning oceanfront acres, The Del is conveniently located just across the bay from downtown San Diego and its many attractions, and only a 15-minute drive from the San Diego International Airport.
